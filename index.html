A word from our CEO “
We started Almasa with a simple belief — that a sign is the first handshake between a business and its city. Get it right, and people trust you before they’ve even walked in.

When we opened our doors in Dubai, the market was full of vendors who could print fast or fabricate cheap — but few who cared whether the final piece actually made a brand look proud. We chose the harder path: own the craft end to end, treat every shopfront as if it were our own name on the street, and never hand a client a sign we wouldn’t put on our own building.

Today that promise still runs the workshop. Whether you’re a new café in Al Quoz or a tower tenant in DIFC, you get the same obsession with detail, the same respect for Arabic and English alike, and the same team standing behind the work long after it’s installed. Thank you for trusting us to put your name out into the world — we don’t take it lightly.
